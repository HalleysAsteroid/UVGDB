Welcome to UVGDB, the Universal Video Game DataBase! This database is designed to store and organize information about a wide variety of videogames, including details such as the title, release date, region, publisher, and platform. You can use the database to view our whole catalog, or browse through the collection to discover new titles. Whether you're a seasoned gamer or new to the world of videogames, this database is a valuable resource for finding the information you need.

As they say, every instance of UVGDB is personalized, so please feel free to add and remove titles to your heart's content! Whether you're making a list for yourself or to share with friends, we hope you enjoy our services and wish you luck in all your gaming endeavors!
